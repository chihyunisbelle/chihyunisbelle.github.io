Project: Eventure

Click to explore the code
Click for Eventure Demo Video by Julia

a social app designed to help users to explore and share experiences, or find and befriend with users(a.k.a 'eventurers') who share matching or similar palette. Incorporating PostgreSQL, Docker, React, FastAPI and google map API to create an interactive and responsive platform for saving and sharing various experiences


Breaking down the features:

entering the site, without signup or login, the visitor will be greet with the home page and an introduction and instructions of what eventure and evneturers do

by entering the fields, you will become an eventurer and able to add and save your own eventures, befriend with other eventurers or save experiences from other's page

logging in, if you are already an eventurer

when you are logged in, you can add and save your own eventure, by filling out the form

once the eventure is saved, it will be shown on the map, and by clicking the eventure, you will go into the detail page of that eventure

you can explore other eventurer's map pin and send them friend request to get social, the eventurers who have accepted your friend request will be listed in your friendlist
